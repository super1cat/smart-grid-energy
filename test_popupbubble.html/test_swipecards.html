Explore the Benefits

Lower electric bills with no upfront cost ➤
PPAs let you avoid the cost of buying solar panels. You simply buy the electricity they generate at a lower, locked-in rate.
Maintenance and battery replacements are included ➤
If something goes wrong — or when the battery reaches end of life — the electric company takes care of the replacement, free of charge.
You’re switching electric providers, not buying solar ➤
Just like you can choose a different phone provider, you can choose who provides your home’s electricity — even if it comes from your own roof.
