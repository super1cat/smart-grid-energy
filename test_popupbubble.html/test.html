Common Reasons People Choose a PPA

I didn’t want to pay upfront for solar
With a PPA, the solar electric company installs and owns the equipment. You don’t have to pay for panels or batteries — you just buy the electricity.
I just wanted lower rates without the hassle
PPA rates are stable, and usually much lower than traditional utilities. The company monitors and maintains everything — you just pay for the power you use.
It felt like switching cell phone carriers
That’s exactly the idea. You switch to a new electric provider, and they install equipment to generate clean energy right from your home.
