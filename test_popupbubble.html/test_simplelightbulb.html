What Makes This Work

You don’t pay for equipment
The provider installs and owns the solar system. You simply buy the electricity it produces at a more affordable rate.
The battery is maintained and replaced if needed
If something goes wrong with the system or the battery wears out, the electric company replaces it at no cost to you.
You’re just switching electric companies
Instead of buying electricity from your utility, you get it from a company that installs solar and delivers power directly from your roof.
