Clear Answers in Sticky Balloons

Why don’t I pay for the solar system?
Because the system is installed and owned by the PPA electric company. You only pay for the power it generates — not the equipment itself.
What if something breaks or stops working?
Maintenance and battery replacements are included in the agreement. You don’t need to schedule service or pay out of pocket — it’s already covered.
Do I still get power if it’s cloudy or dark?
Yes — your system includes a modern battery that stores power for overnight and cloudy days. You’ll always have consistent electricity.
