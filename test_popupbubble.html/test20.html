Here's How It Works

You don’t pay for the system

The solar equipment is installed and owned by the electric company. You only pay for the electricity it produces — not for the panels, batteries, or maintenance.

Maintenance is fully covered

Monitoring, repairs, and even battery swaps are all handled for you. No surprise bills. No service scheduling. Just power, backed by full coverage.

You’re just switching providers

Instead of buying electricity from your old utility, you now get it directly from the solar system on your home — at a lower, fixed rate. Same lights, better power source.
