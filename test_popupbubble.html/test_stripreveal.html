What Makes a PPA So Different?

You don’t pay for the system — just the electricity
With a PPA, the provider installs and maintains the solar system on your home. You only pay for the power it generates, often at a lower rate than your utility.
Maintenance, monitoring, and battery replacements are included
The PPA electric company is responsible for keeping the system working. If the battery ever fails, they replace it — no added cost, no service call hassle.
Switching providers doesn’t mean buying solar
You don’t have to buy solar panels to switch electric companies. A PPA lets you change who provides your power — it just happens to come from your roof.
