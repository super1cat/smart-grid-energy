Smart Reasons to Go Solar With a PPA

1
Lower your rate, no equipment cost
You get a lower, fixed electric rate — and the solar equipment is installed and maintained by the provider. You don’t pay for panels or batteries up front.
2
They handle maintenance and upgrades
From battery swaps to software updates, the PPA company keeps everything running smoothly. You just enjoy clean, reliable energy.
3
You can switch electric providers
Instead of buying from the utility, you buy electricity from the company that installs solar right on your home. It’s simple, legal, and often way cheaper.
