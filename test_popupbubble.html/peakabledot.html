Quick Clarifications When You Need Them

You don’t pay for the panels, just the electricity
The PPA electric company owns and installs the system. You only pay for the power it produces, not the hardware itself.
Repairs and replacements are covered
If the system breaks or the battery fails, the provider handles all service and replacement at no cost to you.
You’re not buying solar — you’re switching electric providers
Think of it like changing who supplies your electricity. Solar just happens to be where it's coming from now.
