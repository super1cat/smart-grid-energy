Here’s How It Works

You don’t pay for the system
The solar equipment is installed and owned by the electric company. You only pay for the electricity it produces — not for the panels, batteries, or maintenance.
Maintenance is fully covered
Monitoring, repairs, and even battery swaps are all handled for you. No surprise bills. No service scheduling. Just power.
You’re just switching providers
Your new provider generates electricity on your roof with solar, and you buy it at a lower rate. Same wires. Better outcome.
