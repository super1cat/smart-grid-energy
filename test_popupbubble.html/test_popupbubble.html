PPA Benefits in Quick Hints

Most homeowners don’t realize that they can switch electricity providers Instead of buying from your utility, you can get power from a solar provider who installs and maintains the system for you. without buying solar panels.

With a PPA, you don’t own the equipment The electric company owns, monitors, and replaces the system if anything breaks — you just buy the electricity. but you benefit from lower, stable rates.

That also means you avoid the upfront costs No need to pay for solar panels or batteries. The system is installed at no cost, and you just pay for power over time. that come with buying solar.
