Common Questions

Clear answers to the things homeowners ask us every day.
A PPA lets you buy electricity from solar panels installed on your home without owning the equipment. It’s simple, predictable, and usually cheaper than your current utility.
The provider installs, insures, and maintains the solar system for you, including battery replacement. They also monitor performance over time, making sure everything continues to run efficiently so you don’t have to think about a thing.
At the end of your PPA term, you usually have a few options: renew with an updated rate, purchase the system at a discount, or have it removed. Whatever your choice, your provider will guide you through it with plenty of notice.
Not directly. The solar system is not yours to sell, but the fact that your electricity costs are lower can make your home more attractive to buyers.
