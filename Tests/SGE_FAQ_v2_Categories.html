Common Questions

Power Purchase Agreement Basics›
What is a Power Purchase Agreement?
A PPA lets you buy electricity from solar panels installed on your home without owning the equipment.
Who maintains the solar system?
The company providing the electricity installs, insures, and replaces batteries as needed.
Owning Your Solar System›
Can I own my solar system instead?
Yes! We offer options for financing or purchasing your system outright.
Electric Companies – PG&E, SDG&E, and others›
How does this compare to my current electric company?
You’ll pay less for electricity coming from solar panels installed at your home—usually with battery backup.
How Solar and Batteries Work›
How does solar power my home?
Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, which can power your home or be stored in a battery.
General Electricity Questions›
Will I still be connected to the grid?
Yes — your home stays connected for backup or when additional power is needed.
